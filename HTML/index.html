Our App

Have you been at a store and wondered if a bottle of wine was a good vintage? Wines vary drastically from year to year even if they are from the same vineyard. Our app was designed to give you ratings for wine using only the vintage and the appellation for both red and white wines.

We used Machine Learning to determine the best rating of each wine using wines from all over the West Coast. Our app uses weather, wine rankings, and soil data to assist discerning customers when choosing their favorite wine. Using our app can help you find the best vintages across multiple appellations.
